The Process

How it works

  1. 01
    Parent or Individual Consultation

    A conversation with Cal to understand your son, your concerns, and what you're looking for. Parents are always welcome to meet Cal first — in person or online.

  2. 02
    In-Person Introductory Meeting

    Cal meets your son face-to-face. No pressure — just a real conversation to build rapport and see if it's the right fit.

  3. 03
    Join the Founding Cohort

    Once confirmed, your son joins the founding cohort and receives his personalised mentoring plan.

  4. 04
    Weekly Mentorship Sessions

    Structured, consistent sessions — the backbone of real change. Discipline is built through showing up, every week.

  5. 05
    WhatsApp Support & Accountability

    Between sessions, Cal is accessible. Check-ins, encouragement, and accountability — not just during sessions, but through the week.

  6. 06
    Progress Check-Ins

    Regular updates shared with parents. You'll see the growth — in his habits, his mindset, and his confidence.

FAQ

Common questions

What age range is this for?

MM works with young men aged 10 to 18. The mentoring approach adapts to each individual's maturity and stage of development.

Is this one-to-one or group?

The founding cohort is a small, close group with significant individual attention from Cal. Each young man also receives personalised guidance based on his specific needs and goals.

How often are sessions?

Sessions run weekly. Consistency is the foundation of everything we build — showing up every week is non-negotiable.

Can parents speak to Cal first?

Absolutely — and it's encouraged. Every enrolment starts with a parent consultation, either in person or online. You'll meet Cal before your son does.

Is there WhatsApp support?

Yes. Founding cohort members have direct WhatsApp access to Cal for check-ins, accountability, and support between sessions.

What's included in the founding cohort?

Weekly structured sessions, direct WhatsApp access, personal accountability system, progress check-ins with parents, and founding member pricing locked in permanently.

How much does it cost?

Founding cohort pricing is introductory and will increase for future cohorts. Exact pricing is shared during the parent consultation so Cal can explain the full value of what's included.

Where are sessions held?

Sessions are held in person. Specific location details are shared during the consultation process.
