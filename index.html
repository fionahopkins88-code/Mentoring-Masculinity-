Questions you might have

What happens on a discovery call?

We talk for 20 minutes. You tell me what's going on, I ask a few questions, and we figure out together whether mentoring is the right next step. There's no sales pitch.

How old does my son need to be?

I work with young men aged 10 to 18. The approach adapts to their stage — a 10-year-old and a 17-year-old need very different things, and I adjust accordingly.

What if my son doesn't want to do it?

That's normal — and it's fine. I'm used to meeting resistance with patience. Most boys come around once they realise I'm not another adult telling them what to do.

How much does it cost?

We'll cover that on the discovery call once I understand what your son needs. Every plan is individual.

Is this therapy?

No. I'm a mentor, not a therapist. If I think your son needs clinical support, I'll tell you honestly and help you find the right person.
