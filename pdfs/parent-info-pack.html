Parent Info Pack

Helping Your Son Build Confidence, Discipline, and Direction

A structured mentoring programme for young men aged 10–18, led by Cal.

What Is MM?

Mentoring Masculinity (MM) is a structured mentoring programme designed to help young men develop the confidence, discipline, and emotional resilience they need to thrive. Through regular 1-to-1 and small group sessions, Cal works directly with your son to build accountability, self-respect, and a clear sense of direction.

MM is not therapy. It's not a boot camp. It's consistent, intentional mentorship from someone who shows up every week and holds the standard.

Who Is Cal?

Cal is the founder of MM and the lead mentor. He's a coach, role model, and trusted masculine guide who leads with both strength and empathy. Cal founded MM because too many young men are growing up without structure, accountability, or a strong male presence to learn from.

Every parent has the opportunity to meet Cal in person before their son begins. Your trust matters — and Cal earns it face-to-face.

Who Is This For?

Lacking Confidence

Young men who second-guess themselves, avoid challenges, or shrink in social situations.

Needing Direction

Drifting without goals or purpose. No ambition, no plan, no fire.

Struggling With Discipline

Can't stick to routines, follow through, or hold themselves accountable.

Missing Masculine Guidance

No consistent, strong male role model. Cal fills that gap as a trusted guide.

Young Athletes

Physical talent but mental blocks — performance anxiety, lack of focus.

Navigating Identity

Working out who they are, what they stand for, and who they want to become.

What Parents Can Expect

  • In-person parent meeting with Cal before any commitment
  • A real consultation about your son's specific needs
  • Regular progress updates and communication
  • A safe, structured, accountable environment
  • Clear expectations and measurable progress
