Programme Overview

What MM Looks Like In Practice

A structured, consistent mentoring programme built around weekly sessions, accountability, and real progress.

How It Works

1
Parent or Individual Consultation

A conversation with Cal to understand your son, your concerns, and what you're looking for. Parents are always welcome to meet Cal first — in person or online.

2
In-Person Introductory Meeting

Cal meets your son face-to-face. No pressure — just a real conversation to build rapport and see if it's the right fit.

3
Join the Founding Cohort

Once confirmed, your son joins the founding cohort and receives his personalised mentoring plan.

4
Weekly Mentorship Sessions

Structured, consistent sessions — the backbone of real change. Discipline is built through showing up, every week.

5
WhatsApp Support & Accountability

Between sessions, Cal is accessible. Check-ins, encouragement, and accountability — not just during sessions, but through the week.

6
Progress Check-Ins

Regular updates shared with parents. You'll see the growth — in his habits, his mindset, and his confidence.

What MM Builds

  • Confidence and self-belief
  • Discipline and consistency
  • Routine and structure
  • Emotional resilience
  • Masculine leadership
  • Accountability
  • Communication skills
  • Physical and mental performance
  • Self-respect
  • Future direction and purpose

What Makes MM Different

Consistency Over Intensity

This isn't a weekend boot camp. It's weekly, structured mentorship that compounds over time. Real change takes consistency.

Built on Relationship

Cal builds genuine trust with every young man. The mentorship works because the relationship is real — not transactional.

Parent Partnership

Parents aren't left in the dark. You'll meet Cal, receive progress updates, and be part of the process from start to finish.

Personalised Approach

Every young man is different. Cal adapts his approach to your son's age, personality, challenges, and goals.
